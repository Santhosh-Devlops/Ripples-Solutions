About Ripples Solutions

Ripples Solutions, founded in 2003, is a trusted IT Systems Integrator serving Southern Tamil Nadu. We provide end-to-end IT infrastructure solutions, acting as a single point of contact for desktops, laptops, servers, networking, security, storage, and data center products.

Vision: To be a leader in systems integration, recognized for exceptional customer support and reliable technology solutions.

Mission: To deliver innovative, high-quality IT solutions that improve productivity, streamline operations, and support business growth.

Authorized Partnerships

  • Regional Sub Distributor for Lenovo Commercial Business
  • Gold Partner for Sophos Firewall
  • Gold Partner for Cyberoam Firewall
  • D-Link Distributor for South Tamil Nadu

Growth Strategy: Market expansion, service diversification, and strategic partnerships with leading technology providers.
